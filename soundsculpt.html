Brand Statement

SSF is a music festival in Austin, TX. that celebrates diverse sounds and vibrant beats, uniting music enthusiasts.

Project Background

The website allows music enthusiasts to get tickets, look up activities and vendors at the event, check the list of artists participating, and search for additional information.

Objective

This project aims to reach a bigger audience outside of the US and bring together all people who enjoy music to have a great time.

Attitude

Fosters an inclusive and positive atmosphere, encouraging a sense of unity, creativity, and shared joy among attendees while prioritizing safety, respect, and an appreciation for diverse musical experiences.
