For the 44th BBQ Festival, I designed a detailed, vibrant poster that captures the event's lively atmosphere. The colorful elements and intricate details evoke a classic BBQ celebration's mouthwatering flavors and excitement. The design process started with a series of hand-drawn sketches, where I played with various concepts and layouts to create a poster that reflects the essence of the festival while grabbing attention with bold, eye-catching visuals.

<

Every element of the poster is thoughtfully placed to draw viewers in and communicate the festival's spirit. The bright, contrasting colors were chosen to evoke the heat and energy of BBQ, while the intricate details tell the story of a beloved community tradition. The result is a poster that's as flavorful and memorable as the event.
